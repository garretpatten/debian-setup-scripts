# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Assume good intent; disagree with technical ideas, not people.
- Prefer concrete reproduction steps, logs, and script paths so others can act on reports.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized language or attention, sustained disruption, doxing, publishing private information without permission, or any conduct inappropriate in a professional setting is prohibited.

## Reporting

Contact [garret.patten@proton.me](mailto:garret.patten@proton.me). Reports are read in confidence; the maintainer will respond as promptly as practical.

## Enforcement

Moderation (including removing content or limiting access to the repository) may be used against repeat or serious misconduct.
